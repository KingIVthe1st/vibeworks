Common Questions

What makes VibeWorks different from typical dev shops?

We combine technical execution with built-in distribution. Most agencies hand you a product and wish you luck finding users. We partner with creators who bring audiences, or build with launch strategy baked in from day one.

How do you deliver AI products in 3-6 weeks?

We specialize in rapid prototyping with production-ready foundations. Our team has built this exact workflow 8 times. We know where to move fast (MVP features) and where to invest (scalable architecture). You get a real product, not a demo.

What's the difference between Partnership and Development Services?

Partnership Track is for creators with existing audiences (50K+ followers). We build your AI product idea in exchange for revenue share‚Äîzero upfront cost. Development Services are for companies who need AI product development with fixed pricing and full ownership.

Do you only work on consumer AI products?

No. While our portfolio showcases consumer products built with creators, we take on B2B AI development, internal tools, and enterprise prototypes. The common thread is rapid development and market-ready delivery.

What happens after the initial build?

For partnerships, we remain involved through ongoing revenue share. For development services, we offer maintenance retainers or transition the codebase to your team with full documentation. You choose the path that fits your needs.

How do you evaluate partnership opportunities?

We look for creators with engaged audiences (not just follower counts), clear alignment between their niche and product idea, and commitment to active promotion. If your audience trusts your recommendations, we're interested.
